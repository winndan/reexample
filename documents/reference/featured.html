Our Story

Real Estate That Puts You First

Real estate isn’t just about properties — it’s about people. M+ Properties was founded by Mary Gatdula with one clear goal: to make buying, selling, leasing, or investing simple, empowering, and always centered on you.


After years of working with clients, Mary saw that people needed more than transactions. They wanted a trusted guide, honest advice, and someone who stayed present even after the deal was done. That insight became the foundation of M+ Properties.


The “+” in M+ Properties stands for everything beyond the basics — clarity, consistency, and true partnership. With us, you get more than a transaction. You get a long-term partner committed to making every step of your real estate journey transparent, reliable, and client-centered.

Mary Gatdula

Founder of M+ Properties

Founder & Lead Broker, M+ Properties
